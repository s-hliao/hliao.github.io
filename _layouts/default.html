Henry Liao

I'm a Master's student at the Robotics Institute, part of the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I study deployable adaptive control algorithms, and am currently advised by Dr. John Dolan and mentored by Maitham Al-Sumni .

I completed by BS in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2025. During my undergraduate studies, I co-founded ByteFight, a student organization dedicated to hosting large-scale Game AI competitions for students. I also conducted some brief research projects exploring adaptive MPC policies under the mentorship of Jason Gibson and Sasha Lambert.

During my studies I worked as a TA for CS2200 Systems and Networking at Georgia Tech, and 16299 Introduction to Feedback Control Systems at CMU.
